Example 3

Ques. $A = [\table 2,3;-1,2]$ find $A^2$ and $A^3$.

$A^2$ = $A.A$ = $[\table 2,3;-1,2]$ $[\table 2,3;-1,2]$
= $[\table 2\x2-3\x1,2\x3+3\x2;-1\x2-1\x2,-1\x3+2\x2]$
= $[\table 1,12;-4,1]$
$A^3 = A^2 .A$ = $[\table 1,12;-4,1]$ $[\table 2,3;-1,2]$
= $[\table 1\x12-12\x1,1\x3+12\x2;-4\x2-1\x1,-4\x3+2\x1]$
= $[\table -10,27;-9,-10]$
